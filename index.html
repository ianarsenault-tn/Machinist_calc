Measurement Over Wires (60° Threads)

M is the micrometer reading across the three wires. If you don’t know M, set Mode to “Solve M from Pitch Diameter (E)” and enter E.
Formula: M = E + 3W − (√3/2)·P and E = M − 3W + (√3/2)·P.
Used when snapping “best wire” to closest available size.
Best wire (theoretical): W ≈ P/√3 ≈ 0.57735·P
If you don’t know E, you can use a “basic” estimate, but tolerances are not applied.
This is the value you would measure with a mic over the three wires.
